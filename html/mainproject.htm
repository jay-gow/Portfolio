Automatic Self Cleaning Toilet System

Abstract

In today’s world with the ever-increasing population, hygiene is a top priority. With the “Swachh Bharat Abhiyan” in full swing, our Smart Toilet project uses sensor management (smell, IR, ultrasonic, and color sensors) to monitor and automate the cleaning process. The system manages toilet seat operations, monitors cleanliness and sweeper activity, and helps conserve water.
